## Strategic position (after surveying the field)
Agent-payment **rails** are a red ocean ‚Äî agentic.market (x402/Base, ~$51M cumulative),
pay.sh (Solana), Orthogonal (YC, ex-Coinbase), Exa. All settle on *"is this payment
authorized?"*. **None** answer *"is the requesting agent compromised?"*. That gap ‚Äî
**integrity** ‚Äî is the open lane and the only defensible play. Vineland sits **above**
every rail as the integrity layer, not as another rail. Rail take-rate is dead
(~$26/day industry-wide); monetize the integrity attestation, chain-agnostically.
Defensibility = **ship-first + detection depth** (Bluewave-security), not the gate
primitive (copyable).
